Participant Information

The registration fee per participant is USD350 (Early Bird), USD 500 (Regular Registration) and USD 600 (Late Registration).

The registration fee covers the total cost of the program over 3 nights and 4 days; all meals and accommodation, learning as well as presentation sessions, program materials. However, the fee does not cover airfares, accommodation and food before and after the event, meals that are not provided by us, personal expenses and Visa fees.

Participants are responsible for seeking their own financial assistance to support their attendance at GEB-Mega Edition. For the details about fundraising, please visit the funding section. Participants are responsible for checking to see if they need a visa to travel to Indonesia and apply for one if needed. GEB can help the applicant by providing a support letter, once the registration fees have been paid. For the details about the visa, please visit the Visa section.

Applications are considered on a rolling basis (first come, first served basis) and the deadline is as mentioned below

  1. Early Bird: 31st March, 2018
  2. Normal Registration: 30th April, 2018
  3. Late Registration: 20th June, 2018

Scholarship

Global Entrepreneurship Bootcamp will provide few scholarships (merit based) to the participants. The scholarship will be provided in the registration fee. For the scholarship, the participants will be judged on the basis of their Business Idea.

There is special discount in the registration fee for the participants from Indonesia.

Individual Application

In our previous Bootcamp we had around 80 participants from over 20 countries and many had applied individually. You need to fill the application form below to apply as an individual and need not fill the name of your team members.

During the event you need to work with the team you are assigned. Once you have applied, you will receive a conformation e-mail which means that we have received your application and is under consideration. We will let you know if we need any more information from you. If your application is accepted, you are required to pay the registration fee within the given deadline.

Group Application

If you wish to participate as a group then you need to fill the application form below with the details of your team members. A team leader can apply on the behalf of the entire team and make sure to fill all the details and include the names of your team member correctly. There should be minimum of 3 members in the team and maximum of 6 members. If you are applying as a team then you can work on your own idea and need not get mixed with other delegates.

In case if you are applying as the representative of any organization and have not finalized the names of your team members then please mention it clearly on the application form and also mail us about it.

Once you have applied, you will receive a confirmation e-mail which means that we have received your application and is under consideration. We will let you know if we need any more information from you. If your application is accepted, you are required to pay the registration fee within the given deadline.
