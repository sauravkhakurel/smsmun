Code Of Conduct

Introduction

(For special attention of the Delegate)
The delegates are required to agree with the following code of conduct. Organizers will enforce this code throughout the event. We expect cooperation from all to help ensure a safe environment for everybody in these three days. Please read the document carefully. Any breaching or violation of the Code of Conduct will result in disqualification.

Delegate Rules and Regulations

  1. All delegates are expected to behave courteously and professionally throughout the conference and follow the Dress code of MUN.
  2. Strict regularity, punctuality, implicit obedience, courtesy in words and deeds, cleanliness in dress and person is expected from every delegate.
  3. Attending all workshops and all three days of the conference is a must although unavoidable circumstances are an exception.
  4. Irregular attendance, habitual lack of interest during the committee session, stealing, misconduct inside the conference’s premises, willful repeated breaches of the SMS MUN regulations are sufficient reasons for the dismissal of the delegate.
  5. Any reports or objectionable conduct on the part of any delegate will make them liable for disciplinary action.
  6. All delegates should be particularly careful not to litter the environment and use bins and waste paper baskets provided for this purpose.
  7. Drinking alcoholic beverages, smoking, doing drugs etc are strictly prohibited during the conference days.
  8. Abusive behavior, be it sexual, physical or verbal shall not be conducted and strict actions will be taken against it.
  9. If found with harmful or threatening objects, they will be confiscated and the owner will be dealt with severely.
  10. On the basis of the intensity of the offense committed by the delegate, an official complaint letter will be issued to the head of their Institution and Guardians.
  11. Creating public disturbance of engaging in any sort of illegal activities is highly unacceptable.
  12. All the delegates will be required to abide by the mentioned rules and are expected to follow any further instructions provided to them by the SMSMUN organizing committee.
