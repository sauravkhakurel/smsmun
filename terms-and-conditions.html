Terms & Conditions

Global Entrepreneurship Bootcamp-Mega Edition is an intensive educational/leadership session that brings together many young entrepreneurs from around the world and places them on a common platform to innovate and create new businesses by collaboration. The Bootcamp is designed to groom the innovations and new ideas into startups. Global Entrepreneurship Bootcamp-Mega Edition is a part of Global Entrepreneurship Bootcamp owned and operated by OYA Opportunities, a subsidiary of OYA Inc.

The following terms and conditions are the basis of the contract between the participant and GEB (OYA Inc.), so please read them carefully.

  1. Online Application

    Once you are ready and have confirmed all the requirements and are sure of your commitments, please go to (Application link) and complete the information for all fields. Applications are subjected to approval and will be accepted only after the team has reviewed your application. Our team will contact you by e-mail you have provided in the application form.

    If you get selected, you will receive a confirmation mail with the payment details. Make sure to pay the fee within the given deadline. In case if you fail to pay the fee then your spot will be given to the other potential applicant.

  2. Participant Code of Conduct
    1. Attend all the sessions and be punctual for the event.
    2. Be respectful during the entire event.
    3. In case if you have any trouble please contact the GEB-team members directly. We are always happy to help.
    4. Provide an application or notice mentioning clearly the reason for your absence to the GEB-team member.
    5. Behave in a sociable manner, be polite and make an effort to be friendly and talk to people so that you can grow your connections.
    6. Never leave the venue on your own, make sure you are with someone and also inform the team member whenever you leave the venue.
    7. Be careful about what you are eating. Consume the alcohol responsibly and only if you are over 18. GEB has a strict NO DRUGS policy.
    8. The participant shall be responsible to any damage to the properties of GEB or the Bootcamp venue will be subjected to fine.
    9. A participant shall not impair, obstruct or interfere during the event and in the schedule.

    1. Early Registration : USD 350
    2. Normal Registration : USD 500
    3. Late Registration : USD 600

    The registration fee covers the total cost of the program over 3 nights and 4 days; all meals and accommodation, learning as well as presentation sessions, program materials. However, the fee does not cover airfares, accommodation and food before and after the event, meals that are not provided by us, personal expenses and Visa fees.

    1. The registration fee is non-refundable.
    2. The fee must be paid within the given deadline
    3. There are no cost breakdowns. The fee is the full package that you need to take.
    4. We do not accept partial payments.
    5. GEB will not be responsible for any bank charges. Please ensure the full amount reaches us.

    Please note that the accommodation provided is in shared basis with same gender, using twin rooms.
