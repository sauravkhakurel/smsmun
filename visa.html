Visa

Visa

For many nationalities who wish to enter Indonesia, visas are issued on arrival at major airports in Indonesia. The tourist visas might be extendable but are not changeable into other types of visas. The visa facilities are available at the Immigration check-point.

On the basis of passport and citizenship, following are the most common options you can choose when you come to Indonesia:

  1. Free Visa (more than 140 countries (FREE entry, 30 days valid NOT extendable)
  2. Visa on Arrival (costs 35US$, 30 days valid, can be extended (once for 30 days)
  3. Social, Tourist or cultural Visa (B-211) (can stay 60 days, can be extended 3 times for 30 days each, issued by consulate or embassy outside Indonesia)
  4. Multiple Entry Visa (stay up to 60 days per visit, valid 1 year, issued by consulate or embassy outside Indonesia)

For more details about the Indonesian Visa requirements for your country, contact the Indonesian Embassy or Consulate nearby your place.
