St. Mary's MUN

About St. Marys MUN

After witnessing the tremendous success of the SMSMUN’17, St.Mary’s Model United Nations Club was officially established this year, which is the only club of St.Mary’s School that is independently run by the students.

Keeping in mind to give more students the opportunity to participate in the conference, the SMSMUN Club has decided to organize two conferences this year: Intra-School SMSMUN’18 and Inter-School SMSMUN’18. The Intra- School SMSMUN and the Inter-School SMSMUN will be a three-day conference which will be held in the premises of St.Mary’s School from July 20 through July 22, 2018 and from August 3 through August 5, 2018 respectively.

SMSMUN serves as a platform to help students enhance and reinforce their knowledge; of what they learn in the classroom, of the world’s matters, and of the workings of the civil society’s mindset about it. It serves as a medium of acquainting the civil society with the functions of human relations, by sharing one’s perspective and policies. It will foster as guidance in research, public speaking, and teamwork-the skills that one will need throughout their career.

Thus, in a small way, SMSMUN serves to fulfill the aims and goals set out by the founders of the United Nations in the Preamble of the Charter: “to practice tolerance and live together in peace with one another as good neighbors,”

St. Mary's School

St.Mary’s High School, Kathmandu, was established in 1955. It is under the management of the Sisters of The Congregation of Jesus founded by an English lady, Mary Ward, in 1609. It is an international institution especially devoted to the education of youth. This institution is open to girls of all communities, irrespective of status,caste, creed and religion. The school imparts not only academic educations to girls, but also helps them to form an integrated personality by developing in them a strong character, love of truth, respect, loyalty, a sense of justice and a source of happiness and blessing to all with whom they come in contact.
