Funding


Sometimes, despite your deep interest and enthusiasm to attend the event, financial aspect can play a major aspect to stop you. But based on our previous experience we can tell that Fund Raising can help at times. There are participants who have participated collecting fund for the airfare as well as registration fee. All you need is to give it a try as we are unable to offer funding to the participants. There are some ways that can help you raise fund.

  1. First you need to get an official letter from us which can help you search a sponsor.
  2. It is always better to start as early as possible and try to find as many options as possible.
  3. Approach your school/university, the organization you are affiliated to, the government, local organizations, business personals, international people, some NGOs and INGOs.
  4. Crowdfunding can also be helpful.
  5. Write to various possible sponsors including your interest, enthusiasm and need.
