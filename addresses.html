Message From The Secretary General

Esteemed Delegates and EB members,

It is my distinct honour to cordially invite you all to the second edition of the External Chapter of St.Mary’s Model United Nations conference (SMSMUN) which will be a three-day conference starting from 3rd of August through 5th of August,2018.

The world today is in a dire state with its countless challenges which demand immediate action. Despite this, we are not doing enough to restore the peace and harmony in the world. It is important to remember that every action, no matter how small or big, that we take upon to make this world a better place counts and SMSMUN is one such endeavour which introduces young people to the realities and complexities of international relations.

St.Mary’s School MUN brings to you 7 dynamic committees, each with an intriguing agenda selected while keeping in mind the urgent issues that need to be addressed.

As we endeavour to shape the perceptions of tomorrow’s leaders, we expect to see truly diplomatic debates in intense, pressure packed environment that would be testing the composure, as well as impulsiveness of the delegates, while they strive to reach real life solutions to global issues.

Warm regards,
Shreya Khakurel
Secretary General

Message from the Principal

Dear Students, Teachers, Parents and Well wishers,

SMSMUN which stands for St.Mary’s School Model United Nations is a student-centered activity. It was started in the year 2017 with the initiative of our out-going students of class 12. It is planned and managed entirely by the students of SMSMUN Executive Committee.

In this era of globalization, learning about the world is more important than ever. No matter what fields of profession students enter, they will interact with people from different countries and diverse backgrounds. Global and International relationships become very important today in the present scenario. In such a situation it becomes very important that students are aware of the challenges that the world faces today especially in the areas of climate change, global health, counter-terrorism, war, refugees, migration,etc.

SMSMUN provides a platform to students to expand their knowledge on global issues and contribute to solve the problems that affect the planet today. The conferences are opportunities for students to internalize what they learn in the class and deliver that knowledge through different debates and discussions. Research, public speaking, teamwork, negotiations and writing skills necessary for active participation in the conferences will develop self-confidence and enhance leadership skills.

I hope that SMSMUN becomes an enjoyable learning experience to students, as they make friends, learn new skills combined with respect, open-mindedness and co-operation and transform themselves into efficient world leaders for the future.

Yours sincerely,
Sr.Mariette CJ
